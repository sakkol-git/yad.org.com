Dormitory and Youth Training Program (DYTP)

The Dormitory and Youth Training Program (DYTP) provides safe housing, leadership training, and academic support for underprivileged university students in Phnom Penh. Through structured workshops on life skills, financial literacy, and career development, DYTP fosters future leaders who engage in community projects, empowering both themselves and their communities.

Learn More

Empowering Youth through Digital Innovation & IT Literacy

This program enhances digital skills among Cambodian youth through technology-based education. By providing laptops with educational resources, training teachers, and mentoring students for national exams, we bridge the digital divide, preparing youth for modern careers and fostering educational equity.

Learn More

Slum Community Education Initiative

Our Slum Community Education Initiative delivers academic support, English classes, and life skills education to underserved urban communities. University students serve as mentors, providing tutoring and health awareness programs, creating a sustainable cycle of empowerment and community development.

Our Story

Founded in response to Cambodia’s educational challenges post-Khmer Rouge, YAD addresses systemic inequalities by empowering underprivileged youth. With a focus on higher education access, digital literacy, and community development, we create pathways for young Cambodians to become leaders and change-makers.

Our Mission

YAD is committed to expanding educational access through technological innovation, removing barriers with scholarships and dormitories, and fostering leadership by engaging youth in community service. We aim to build a sustainable cycle of education and empowerment.

Our Vision
